## About Google DeepMind

Google DeepMind is a team of scientists and engineers advancing artificial intelligence for widespread public benefit and scientific discovery, with safety and ethics as core priorities.

## About the Role

The Robotics team seeks research scientists to develop safe humanoid robot capabilities. The position emphasizes embodied AI that benefits people in physical environments, focusing on agent reasoning, human-robot interaction, and physical safety using vision-language-action models.

## Responsibilities

- Design, implement, train, and evaluate large models and algorithms for humanoid robots
- Develop algorithmic advances for robots' self-understanding and VLA capabilities
- Create learned policies for human-appropriate responses and handling atypical situations
- Write software for rapid research iteration
- Participate in diverse areas including simulation learning, reinforcement learning, transformers, video generation, and robot control
- Collaborate effectively within fast-paced teams pursuing ambitious research goals
- Generate creative ideas, conduct experiments, and communicate findings internally and externally

## Requirements

- PhD in a technical field or equivalent practical experience
- Knowledge of current large machine learning research
- Experience working with real-world robots

## Preferred Qualifications

- Research background in humanoid whole body control, vision language action models, motion planning, force control, AI safety, diffusion policies, world models, imitation learning, reinforcement learning, sim-to-real transfer, or alignment techniques
- Expertise applying large datasets with deep neural networks to make robots functional
