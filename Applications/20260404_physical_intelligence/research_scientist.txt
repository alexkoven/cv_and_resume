Research Scientist
We look for research scientists with a proven record of research accomplishments in robotics, machine learning, computer vision, and other related fields. If you enjoy building systems and bring a solid foundation in engineering, you’ll thrive in this role. That said, we’re not just looking for the conventional path — we’re excited about people who can make an impact, no matter their background.

Pursuant to the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance, we will consider for employment qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records.
