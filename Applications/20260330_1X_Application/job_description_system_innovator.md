## About 1X

Founded in 2015, 1X Technologies develops advanced humanoid robots for household applications. The organization emphasizes vertical integration, designing and manufacturing all components in-house, from motor coils to AI systems. 1X Labs aims to provide the science and technology that enables human-level, general-purpose humanoids.

## About the Role

This is an open call for exceptionally technical individuals with concrete ideas, prototypes, or research that could advance humanoid performance toward human-level capability. The position suits those with years of deep technical work that hasn't reached full realization — whether due to resource constraints, organizational limitations, or incomplete development.

## Requirements

Candidates must demonstrate at least one of the following:

- First-author or equivalent high-impact scientific publication in a relevant technical field
- A prototype or system demonstrably advancing the state of the art
- Founding or co-founding experience in technical ventures with meaningful external capital (>$3M) or deep-technology product deployment
- Comparable concrete technical track record proving ability to carry ideas through to implementation

## Application Focus Areas

Applicants should pitch:

- The specific performance gap targeted for improvement
- Existing technical foundation (idea, prototype, or research)
- Why current approaches fall short
- Initial building and testing priorities with unrestricted resources
