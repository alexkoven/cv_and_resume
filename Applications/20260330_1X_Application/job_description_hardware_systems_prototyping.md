## About 1X

Founded in 2015, 1X focuses on developing advanced humanoid robots for household use. The mission centers on creating an abundant labor supply through safe, intelligent humanoids that approach human-level performance in elegance, efficiency, and dexterity.

## About the Role

This position involves building real systems rapidly and rigorously while maintaining constant contact with reality. The engineer ideates mechanisms and architectures, translates concepts into working hardware, and subjects prototypes to rigorous testing. Work emphasizes moving fluidly between mechanics, electronics, and software while benchmarking against biological reference targets.

## Responsibilities

- Research and evaluate state-of-the-art technologies in hardware, actuation, and sensing
- Rapidly prototype concepts using electronics, mechanics, and software
- Design experiments to rigorously evaluate performance and characterize limits
- Benchmark prototypes against explicit performance targets
- Collaborate with engineering and manufacturing teams on product integration pathways
- Translate successful prototypes into products that survive real manufacturing constraints

## Requirements

- Strong hands-on background in hardware systems from work or projects
- Lifelong tinkerer mentality with demonstrated experience building and modifying physical systems
- Several years of innovative hands-on work in industry, startups, or research labs
- Portfolio showcasing deep technical ownership beyond basic prototyping
- PCB design and assembly for rapid prototyping
- CAD and mechanical fabrication for fast iteration
- Mill and lathe operation
- Electronics debugging with oscilloscopes and logic analyzers
- Low-level driver, control, and test software development
- System behavior modeling for design guidance
- Experimental design and results analysis
