# Deep Learning Manipulation Engineer — Skild AI

**Company:** Skild AI  
**Location:** Pittsburgh, PA and San Mateo, CA  
**Salary:** $100,000 – $300,000 USD  
**Source:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/skildai-careers/jobs/4408092008  
**Date Added:** 2026-04-11

---

## Overview

Skild AI seeks engineers to develop machine learning models enabling robots to perform complex autonomous tasks. The company focuses on "general purpose robotic intelligence that is robust and adapts to unseen scenarios."

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and optimize deep learning models for robot manipulation tasks
- Model manipulation processes for simulation, analysis, planning, and control systems
- Collaborate across teams on scalable solutions deployable in robotic platforms
- Integrate deep learning models into robotic systems with inference and deployment teams

---

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science, Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, or equivalent experience
- Python proficiency and expertise with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX)
- Advanced knowledge of deep learning architectures, reinforcement learning, and imitation learning
- Experience with distributed deep learning systems

## Application Requirements

Candidates must provide resume, education details, and responses to essay questions about motivation and notable projects.
