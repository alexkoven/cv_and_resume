Foundation is developing the future of general purpose robotics with the goal to address the labor shortage.

Our mission is to create advanced robots that can operate in complex environments, reducing human risk in conflict zones and enhancing efficiency in labor-intensive industries.

We are on the lookout for extraordinary engineers and scientists to join our team. Your previous experience in robotics isn't a prerequisite — it's your talent and determination that truly count.

## Why Are We Hiring for this Role

- Develop and optimize control systems enabling precise, smooth, and safe motion in humanoid robots.
- Bridge AI-generated action models with physical robot execution through robust motion planning, state estimation, and lower-level control architectures.
- Create full-body controllers that integrate action model inputs with real-time feedback, ensuring high performance in complex environments.
- Ensure reliable task execution from manipulation to locomotion while maintaining stability.
- Scale the controls stack across various robot configurations for diverse applications.

## What Kind of Person are we looking For

**Technical Expertise:**
- Strong background in control theory, including linear and nonlinear systems, feedback control, stability analysis, and optimal control methods.
- Proficiency in state estimation techniques (Kalman filters, particle filters, sensor fusion) for estimating robot pose, velocity, and dynamics from noisy sensor data (IMUs, encoders, vision).
- Experience with low-level control strategies: PID controllers, torque control, velocity control, and position control for actuators and joints.
- Skilled in developing full-body controllers that integrate multiple degrees of freedom (DoF), handling kinematics, dynamics, and inverse kinematics.
- Familiarity with optimization techniques for trajectory planning and model predictive control (MPC).
- Proficiency in C++ and Python.
- Experience with robotics middleware (e.g., ROS2) and real-time control systems.
- Real-time operating systems and control frameworks experience highly desirable.

**Experience & Education:**
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in controls engineering, motion planning, or a similar field. Experience from humanoids is ideal but not required; transferable skills from drones, autonomous vehicles, or industrial robotics are welcome.
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field. A PhD is a plus.

**Preferred:**
- Reinforcement learning experience for control policies.
- Hardware-in-the-loop systems integration experience.
- Physical robot troubleshooting and rapid iteration capability.
