# Research Engineer — Skild AI

**Company:** Skild AI  
**Location:** Pittsburgh, PA and San Francisco, CA  
**Salary:** $100,000 – $300,000 USD  
**Source:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/skildai-careers/jobs/4407015008

---

## Overview

Skild AI seeks Research Engineers to develop scalable robotic systems for "general-purpose robotic intelligence that is robust and adapts to unseen scenarios." The company believes machine learning at scale unlocks widespread robot deployment.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Create algorithms for training robot foundation models
- Engineer systems scaling research prototypes into deployable solutions
- Conduct research across perception, robotics, reinforcement learning, and computer vision
- Integrate research insights into practical robotic applications
- Optimize ML models for real-world robotic systems

---

## Required Qualifications

- BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science, Robotics, Engineering, or equivalent experience
- Python proficiency and experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX)
- Published papers or deployed real-world applications
- Technical expertise in deep learning, reinforcement/imitation learning, robotics, or vision
- Robot systems and large-scale model training experience

The company prioritizes "demonstrated abilities and attitude" over industry experience alone.
