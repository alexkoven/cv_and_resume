# Dyna Robotics — Research Engineer, AI Research

## Company
Dyna Robotics makes general-purpose robots powered by a proprietary embodied AI foundation model that generalizes and self-improves across varied environments with commercial-grade performance. Founded by Lindon Gao, York Yang (sold Caper AI for $350M), and Jason Ma (ex-DeepMind). Raised $140M+ (CRV, First Round).

## Position Overview
Research Engineer on the AI Research team — own the end-to-end loop of Embodied AI. Leverage massive "in-the-wild" multimodal datasets to develop models that generalize, self-improve, and operate in unstructured human environments.

## What You'll Do
- **Advance the Frontier:** Design and implement state-of-the-art robot learning algorithms (RL, Imitation Learning, Diffusion) for complex, high-DOF manipulation tasks.
- **VLA & Video Generation:** Architect and scale multi-modal foundation models (VLMs/VLAs) and leverage generative video architectures for world-modeling and robot foresight.
- **End-to-End Ownership:** Own the full research-to-production pipeline: task definition, data curation, large-scale training, and real-time deployment on physical hardware.
- **Physical-World Evaluation:** Evaluate and iterate on models using the robot fleet in dynamic customer environments (beyond benchmarks).
- **Collaborative Leadership:** Work at the intersection of AI, Hardware, and Teleop teams to enhance "Intelligence-per-Watt" of robotic systems.

## What You'll Bring
- PhD (preferred) or Master's in CS/Robotics with publications at RSS, CoRL, ICRA, NeurIPS, CVPR, or ICLR
- 5+ years in AI and robotics research; deploying deep learning models onto physical robots (not just simulation)
- Hands-on experience with VLA models, Video Generation, or large-scale transformer architectures
- Python and PyTorch (or JAX); familiarity with MuJoCo, Isaac, or Drake
- Production-ready research code that scales across GPU clusters

## Bonus Points
- Founding-stage startup experience or leading small, elite research teams
- Classical robotics (controls, state estimation) to complement learning-based approaches
- Profiling and optimizing foundation models for low-latency inference on edge devices
