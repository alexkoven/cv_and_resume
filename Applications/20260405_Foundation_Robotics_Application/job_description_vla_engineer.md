# Foundation Robotics — VLA/Robotics ML Engineer

**Company:** Foundation Robotics  
**Website:** https://foundation.bot  
**Location:** San Francisco (in-person)  
**Salary:** $150,000 – $300,000

## Mission

Foundation is developing general-purpose robots to address the labor shortage — reducing human risk in conflict zones and enhancing efficiency in labor-intensive industries.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and optimize vision-language-action models (transformers, diffusion models, multimodal encoders/decoders)
- Build representations for 2D/3D perception, affordances, scene understanding, and spatial reasoning
- Integrate LLM-based reasoning with action planning and control policies
- Design datasets for multimodal learning: video-action trajectories, instruction following, teleoperation data, and synthetic data
- Interface VLAM outputs with real-time robot control stacks (navigation, manipulation, locomotion)
- Implement grounding layers converting natural language instructions into symbolic, geometric, or skill-level action plans
- Deploy models on on-board or edge compute platforms (latency, safety, reliability)
- Build scalable pipelines for ingesting, labeling, and generating multimodal training data
- Create simulation-to-real (Sim2Real) training workflows using synthetic environments and teleoperated demonstration data
- Optimize training pipelines, model parallelism, and evaluation frameworks
- Collaborate with robotics, hardware, controls, and safety teams
- Participate in user and field testing

## Requirements

- Strong experience with training multimodal models: VLAs, VLMs, vision transformers, LLMs
- Ability to build and iterate on large-scale training pipelines
- Deep proficiency in PyTorch or JAX, distributed training, GPU acceleration
- Strong software engineering skills in Python and modern ML tooling
- Experience with (synthetic) dataset creation and curation
- Understanding of real-time deployment constraints on embedded hardware
- Familiarity with robotics simulation environments (Isaac Lab, MuJoCo, or similar) — preferred
- Hands-on experience with robotics, embodied AI, or reinforcement/imitation learning — preferred
- MSc or PhD in CS, Robotics, ML, or related field (or equivalent industry experience)
